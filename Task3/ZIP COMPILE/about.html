Donations are a type of voluntary gift made by people, businesses, or other organisations to support a range of causes and projects. It is an effective philanthropic instrument that enables people to have a beneficial impact on society. Donations are frequently made to nonprofit organisations committed to certain causes and can take the form of cash, checks, internet transfers, or in-kind contributions. These organisations, which operate under a tax-exempt status, guarantee accountability and openness in the handling of the finances. Individuals and organisations play a critical role in bringing about positive change and improving the lives of others through allocating contribution funds towards education, healthcare, disaster relief, environmental conservation, and other urgent causes.
